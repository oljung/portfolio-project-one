What is Iateria?

Iateria is a high fantasy world rich in history, magic and conflict. Originally created by Joakim Linde in 2019 to be the setting for a campaign in the tabletop role playing game Dungeons and Dragons (DnD for short), the world is equally suitable for a fantasy novel or to host other role playing games. By the summer of 2021 Iateria now hosts three separate games, all with Joakim as dungeon master, and as more players explore the world and the campaigns evolve, so does Iateria itself. This wiki is intended to help you, be you a player, aspiring dungeon master or just a general fantasy enthusiast, to learn more about the rich lore of Iateria.
